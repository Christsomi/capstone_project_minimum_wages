Research Questions

This research investigates how minimum wage policies affect young people aged 16-24, a critical demographic group that often relies on minimum wage jobs for initial labor market experience and income during educational years.

The analysis focuses on understanding both immediate employment effects and broader educational and career development impacts on young workers.

Youth Labor Market Entry & Participation

How do minimum wage policies affect youth (16-24) entry into the labor market?

Key Research Questions:

  • How do higher minimum wages affect youth labor force participation rates?
  • What barriers do young first-time job seekers face in high minimum wage states?
  • How does minimum wage influence youth unemployment duration?
  • Do minimum wage increases affect youth job search behaviors?

Youth Employment Quality & Stability

What are the effects on job quality and stability for workers aged 16-24?

Key Research Questions:

  • How do minimum wages affect youth job turnover rates?
  • What happens to youth part-time employment opportunities when minimum wages increase?
  • Which industries remain most accessible to young workers after minimum wage changes?
  • How do minimum wages affect youth training opportunities in entry-level positions?

Youth Education & Work Decisions

How do minimum wages influence young people's education-work trade-off?

Key Research Questions:

  • Do higher minimum wages affect high school completion rates?
  • How do minimum wages influence college enrollment decisions?
  • What role does minimum wage play in youth work-study choices?
  • How do minimum wages affect youth skill acquisition choices?

Regional Youth Employment

How do local economic conditions affect youth employment under different minimum wages?

Key Research Questions:

  • Do young workers migrate between states for minimum wage differences?
  • How do youth employment rates differ between urban and rural areas?
  • Which industries remain most accessible to youth in different regions?
  • How do local youth unemployment rates respond to minimum wage changes?

Youth Demographic Impacts

Are there differential effects across youth demographic groups?

Key Research Questions:

  • How do minimum wage effects differ between male and female young workers?
  • Does family income background influence youth employment responses?
  • How do minimum wages affect young workers from different educational backgrounds?
  • What role does parental employment status play in youth work decisions?

Long-Term Youth Impact Questions

  • How do early-career minimum wage jobs affect youth career trajectories?
  • What is the impact on youth skill development and human capital accumulation?
  • How does minimum wage exposure during youth affect future earning potential?
  • Do minimum wage policies influence youth industry choices long-term?
  • How do early minimum wage experiences affect youth labor market attachment?
