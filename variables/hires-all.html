Definition

Total Hires measures the total number of individuals hired into employment during a specific time period, serving as a key indicator of labor market activity.

Hiring Count

Includes new hires, rehires, and seasonal workers brought on during the period.

Measurement Period

Typically reported monthly or quarterly, reflecting the total hiring volume.

üî¢

Formula

Total Hires = Number of Employees Added to Payroll During the Period

Calculated by:

  • Counting all individuals newly employed
  • Incorporating data across industries and sectors
  • Excluding internal transfers or promotions

Implications for Youth

Total hires directly impact young workers by influencing their entry into the workforce:

  • Access to internships and entry-level positions
  • Opportunities for skill acquisition and on-the-job training
  • Pathways to career advancement and economic independence
  • Challenges in securing stable employment due to competitive job markets

  • Availability of workforce training programs
  • Educational attainment levels
  • Regional employment opportunities
